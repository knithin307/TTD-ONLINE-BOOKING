Important Information / Rules
  1. Original Photo ID card submitted during booking is mandatory for validation at verification counters.
  2. Pilgrims must strictly report at the scheduled reporting time slot. Late entry is not permitted.
  3. Dress Code Rules: Male pilgrims must wear Dhoti, Shirt, or Kurta Pajama. Female pilgrims must wear Saree or Churidar with Dupatta. Luggage or mobile phones are not permitted in the inner temple line.

General Pilgrimage Rules

1. Entry Dress Code

Tirumala Devasthanams enforces traditional attire inside the temple premises. Pilgrims wearing shorts, jeans, western dresses, mini-skirts, or t-shirts will not be permitted past the Vaikuntam Queue Complex entrance points.

  • Men: Traditional Dhoti / Pancha with upper cloth (Uthareeyam), or Kurta Pajama.
  • Women: Saree, Half-Saree, or Churidar Suit with Dupatta.

2. Identification Verification

The photo ID proof type (e.g. Aadhaar, Voter Card, Passport) and corresponding registration card number selected during booking must be presented in original at reporting gates. Scans, photocopies, or digital versions on unauthorized apps are strictly rejected.

3. Banned & Prohibited Items

Electronic gadgets, cellular phones, smartwatches, cameras, voice recorders, matchboxes, lighters, weapons, leather goods, and baggage are strictly prohibited inside the queues. Please deposit all belongings at free storage centers before reporting.

4. Laddu Prasad Distribution

Additional Laddus purchased during reservation can be claimed at the Laddu Counter Complex (outside the temple exit gate) by scanning the printed barcode receipt.
